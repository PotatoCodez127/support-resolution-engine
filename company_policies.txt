COMPANY POLICY HANDBOOK - ACME ELECTRONICS

SECTION 1: RETURN POLICY
Customers may return unopened items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. 
If the item is opened, it is subject to a 15% restocking fee. 
Defective items can be returned within 90 days for a full replacement or refund. 
We do not accept returns on software, gift cards, or clearance items marked "Final Sale".

SECTION 2: SHIPPING TIMEFRAMES
Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days and is free for orders over $50. 
Expedited shipping takes 2-3 business days and costs $12.99. 
Overnight shipping is available for $29.99 if ordered before 2 PM EST.
International shipping takes 10-21 business days and costs vary by region.

SECTION 3: WARRANTY INFORMATION
All ACME brand electronics come with a standard 1-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects. 
Accidental damage (drops, spills) is not covered unless the customer purchased the "ACME Care+" extended warranty plan.
To file a warranty claim, the customer must provide the original order number and a photo of the defect.
